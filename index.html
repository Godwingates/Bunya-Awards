Shoot With Bunya Awards

Bunya Awards are organized by Mzei promotions With KB electronics in all participating areas of Bunyangabu and are judged by an independent jury set by mzei promotions and KB electronics. Bunya Awards was formed on 28th Nov 2024 in Western Uganda (Rwimi).

How to vote process and Guidelines

    Select a category


    Before voting, you can browse all the award categories and nominees. Visit the “Ongoing” or "Vote Now" section on the Bunya Awards website to get to know the different categories and the nominees for each one. This helps you make informed voting decisions

  • select a nominee


    • When you’re ready to vote, click on the nominee you wish to vote for. There's a vote button and you can vote only once

    1. Review and Confirm

    After voting, you’ll have the chance to review your vote. If everything is correct, your vote will be submitted, and you’ll receive a confirmation message.

      Share and encourage others

    After voting, you can share your participation on social media. Encourage your friends and family to vote and join in the celebration of excellence!
